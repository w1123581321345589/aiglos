**`REPO_TAKEOVER_CHAIN` campaign (confidence 0.97)** ‚Äî the full attacker-becomes-maintainer sequence: T30 SUPPLY_CHAIN (install) ‚Üí T81 PTH_FILE_INJECT (`.pth` write) ‚Üí T04 CRED_HARVEST (credential collection) ‚Üí T41 OUTBOUND_SECRET_LEAK (exfil) ‚Üí T30 again (attacker republishes with stolen GitHub/PyPI token). The Darwin G√∂del Machine Hyperagents architecture (Facebook Research, March 2026) enables agents that modify both their task behavior and the improvement procedure that generates future agents.
